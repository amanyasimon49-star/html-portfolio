Learn More About Me

Hi, I'm Amanya Odweleng. It is my pleasure to welcome you to my page! On this page, I will share everything you might be interested in knowing about me. I have also included links to my contacts, and you are free to reach out to me for any discussion.

I am from South Sudan and a member of the Lopit tribe. I was born in a village called Habirongi in Imehejek Payam. I fled to Kenya during the Sudanese civil war to seek refuge in the Kakuma refugee camp on April 10, 2001.

I am a teacher by profession. I started teaching in 2010, immediately after completing secondary school. Teaching was not the career I chose to pursue, but I had no option but to do it for survival.

In the last few years, I realized I could venture into the world of technology after spending many years as a classroom teacher. So I decided to start learning coding and I can proudly say that it was quite rewarding as can be evident from the site I built since I started.
